Star Health Insurance - Family Health Optima Plan

Policy Name: Family Health Optima
Insurer: Star Health Insurance
Premium: Rs 12000 per year for age 30-40, Rs 18000 for age 40-50
Cover Amount: 5 Lakh, 10 Lakh, 15 Lakh options available

Inclusions:
- Hospitalization expenses including room rent up to Rs 5000 per day
- Pre and post hospitalization for 60 and 90 days
- Day care procedures covered
- Ambulance charges up to Rs 2000
- Organ donor expenses covered

Exclusions:
- Pre-existing conditions waiting period: 24 months for Diabetes, Hypertension
- Maternity expenses not covered in first 2 years
- Cosmetic surgery not covered
- Dental treatment not covered unless due to accident

Sub-limits:
- Room rent: Rs 5000 per day
- ICU charges: Rs 10000 per day
- Cataract surgery: Rs 40000 per eye

Co-pay: 10 percent for members above age 60
Claim Type: Both cashless and reimbursement available
Network Hospitals: 14000 plus hospitals across India including Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities

Suitability:
- Best for families with members aged 25-55
- Good for Tier-2 city residents due to wide hospital network
- Moderate income group 3L to 8L annual income
- Active and moderate lifestyle users benefit from OPD add-on option
- Diabetes patients should note 24 month waiting period

HDFC Ergo Optima Restore Plan

Policy Name: Optima Restore
Insurer: HDFC Ergo
Premium: Rs 9500 per year age 25-35, Rs 14000 age 35-45, Rs 22000 age 45-55
Cover Amount: 3 Lakh, 5 Lakh, 10 Lakh, 15 Lakh, 50 Lakh

Inclusions:
- Restore benefit: Sum insured automatically restored once exhausted
- No claim bonus: 50 percent increase in cover for claim free year
- Hospitalization covered fully
- Mental health treatment covered
- Modern treatments like robotic surgery covered

Exclusions:
- Pre-existing conditions waiting period: 36 months
- First 30 days waiting period for all illnesses except accidents
- Hypertension and Cardiac conditions: 48 month waiting period

Sub-limits:
- No room rent sub-limit - any room category covered
- No sub-limit on doctor fees

Co-pay: Zero co-pay for all ages
Claim Type: Cashless at 13000 plus network hospitals, reimbursement elsewhere

Suitability:
- Excellent for young professionals aged 25-40
- Zero co-pay makes it ideal for Metro city residents
- Higher income group 8L to 15L
- Cardiac patients should note very long 48 month waiting period
- Best lifestyle match: Active and Athlete users

Niva Bupa Reassure 2.0

Policy Name: Reassure 2.0
Insurer: Niva Bupa
Premium: Rs 8000 per year age 18-35, Rs 13000 age 35-50
Cover Amount: 3 Lakh to 1 Crore flexible

Inclusions:
- Lock in feature: premium locked at entry age
- Recharge benefit: sum insured recharged unlimited times
- OPD covered up to Rs 20000 per year
- Teleconsultation unlimited free
- Wellness rewards program

Exclusions:
- Pre-existing conditions: 36 months waiting period
- Asthma: 12 month waiting period
- Obesity treatment not covered

Sub-limits:
- Room rent: single private AC room
- ICU: no sub-limit

Co-pay: 20 percent for Tier-3 city hospitals
Claim Type: Cashless at 10000 plus hospitals

Suitability:
- Best for young users wanting OPD coverage
- Asthma patients benefit from short 12 month waiting period
- Tier-3 city users note 20 percent co-pay applies
- Under 3L income group benefits from affordable premiums
- Sedentary lifestyle users benefit from OPD and teleconsult coverage
